Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an environmental event?

Simply register for an account, click "Create Event" in the navigation, and fill out the event details form. Your event will be visible to the community immediately.

Is EcoConnect free to use?

Yes! EcoConnect is completely free for all users. Our mission is to make environmental action accessible to everyone in the community.

Can I upload photos from events?

Absolutely! You can upload photos from events you've organized or attended. This helps document the positive environmental impact of our community.

How do I join an environmental event?

Browse our events page, find an event you're interested in, and click "Join Event". You'll receive confirmation and can track your participation in your dashboard.
